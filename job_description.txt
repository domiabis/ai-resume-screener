Job Title: Data Analyst

Responsibilities:
- Analyze and interpret complex data sets using SQL and Python (Pandas).
- Create interactive dashboards and visualizations in Power BI.
- Clean, transform, and validate raw data to ensure accuracy.
- Generate actionable business insights through data-driven analysis.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to improve decision-making.

Requirements:
- Strong knowledge of SQL and data manipulation.
- Experience with Power BI or Tableau.
- Familiarity with Python (Pandas, NumPy).
- Analytical mindset and attention to detail.
